33.2 Tiered Service Offerings

  • Starter (Entry): basic auto-reply templates, booking link sharing, calendar sync. Low price to reduce friction.
  • Pro (Core monetisation): full DM booking agent, deposit capture, payment recognition, reminders, Google Sheets CRM.
  • Premium (Studio): multi-staff scheduling, revenue dashboard, API support, priority onboarding & training.

3.3.3 Add-on Services

  • 1:1 Onboarding / Prompt Tuning: pay to have Aiya trained to a tech's tone.
  • Data Migration: moving clients from Acuity/Eresha/Calendly.
  • Custom Branding & SMS credits.
  • Marketing toolkit: AI social captions / promos (future add-on).

3.3.4 Delivery & Support Model

  • Self-service onboarding with optional paid human onboarding.
  • Human-in-loop escalation: ability for tech to take conversation over from AI.
  • Support tiers: email & knowledge base (Starter), in-app chat / onboarding call (Pro/Premium).
